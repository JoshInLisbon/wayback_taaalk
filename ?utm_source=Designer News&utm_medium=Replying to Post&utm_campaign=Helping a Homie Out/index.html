What It's Like To Found & Run Multiple Businesses

With

@dabeeeenster

Ben Rometsch

Ben is the co-founder of many things. In 2002 he founded technical development agency Solid State Group. Together he and his business partner grew the company with no external funding to 14 full time staff.
The duo later went on to found office space group The Hoxton Mix which now provides over 150 desks in three locations in Shoreditch and whose virtual office service is used by thousands of people across the world.
As well as running his core ventures, in recent years Ben has been the technical founder of the following startups: shufflehub.com, getvape.co.uk, buzzmove.com and jigsawbox.com.

How To Think About Chess

With

@RobJHeaton

Robert Heaton

Robert started playing chess when he was 6, and played seriously until the age of 18. His career highlights include 30th in the European U18s, captaining/vice-captaining England at most age groups, playing for Oxford in 3 Varsity matches and achieving rankings of 195 ECF and 2200 FIDE.
He did physics at Oxford then a masters in computer science at Imperial. He is now a software engineer at Stripe and lives in San Francisco. He blogs at robertheaton.com.

Why We Dream & What They Mean

With

PsychotherapyW2

Martin Pollecoff

Martin Pollecoff is a UK Council for Psychotherapy (UKPC) accredited Psychotherapist who has been working with patients since 1977. He has held the position of Chair of the Association of Humanistic Psychotherapists, the chair of The Minster Centre for Psychotherapy and Counselling Training and is currently part of the strategic committee for the UKPC itself.
He has a MSc in Psychotherapy and a Diploma in Clinical Psychotherapy from The Middlesex Metanoia Institute, and a Ma in Change from Surrey University's Human Potential Research Group. psychotherapyw2.co.uk
